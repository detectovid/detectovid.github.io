  • Our Mission

    Our vision is to develop a smartphone app that can be used by everyone in the world to find out in minutes whether they have COVID-19. We are utilizing cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology to identify patterns undetectable by humans from the unique respiratory signatures of lungs damaged by the virus. From extensive research, we believe we can achieve 90% or more accuracy and help control this pandemic!

  • Why This Will Work

    Globally, this idea is being actively researched by several prestigious institutions, including CMU, MIT, and Cambridge. Our differentiator is that we have already started gathering authentic data from hospitals under approved clinic study.

    We are propelled by a swarm of self-motivated and passionate student AI engineers from various institutions. We are growing in number and are driven to serve humanity with no expectation of financial reward.

  • What Can You Expect

    We intend to deploy it globally, saving lives in developing countries, while also preparing the world to handle future pandemics and a potential re-appearance of COVID-19 in November.

  • Our Team

    We are a passionate team of AI engineers, mobile web app developers,Graphic Designers, Marketing specialists and medical professionals. All of us are volunteers and most of us are students under 30. From our hearts, we want to do our best to help the situation and can’t think of anything more noble. We are supported by faculty and professors at our universities as well as industry veterans Silicon Valley.

  • Advantage Of This Model

    1. Billions of people at home confidently knowing they are not infected
    2. Reduced burden on health care systems, equipped with technology to know who to treat first
    3. Eliminated necessity of social distancing
